Deer Park Monastery, San Diego

If you like deers, do not miss a visit to the Deer Park Monastery! The deers here are such dears (pun not intended, I promise.) What a peaceful and beautiful place to visit.

Road Trip to Zion National Park

It was such a beautiful day when we drove to the Zion National Park. The weather was so nice and we got to see all the beautiful colours of this nature park.

Philadelphia Markets

Philly has the cutest markets! There are so many different kinds of good food in Philadelphia. My favourite is, of course, the Philly cheesesteak. Don't miss that!

Assateague State Park

Horses were truly the highlight of my trip to Assateague State Park. Do you see how majestic they look? We were so lucky to have stumbled upon them on our way to the park trail!

Ocean City Pier

It was a gloomy day at the Ocean City pier. It reminded me of a big reality check that summer was over! And that winter would be here in a flash. The waters were calm and it was still a sight to see.

Maryland

Caught a photo of a busy time in the subway. Maryland was a lovely place to visit! We visited the University of Maryland (UMD) and the energy there was unbeliveable.

Jolly Rogers Amusement Park

A pay-as-you-play park that just has the cutest aesthetics! Reminded me of all the old-schooled amusement parks that we used to have.

Zion Stream

The view of this stream was breathtaking in real life! If only you could feel the calmity of the waters and the peacefulness felt in the atmostphere.

San Diego

San Diego is truly somewhere I'd go to retire. Such a cute little town with such adorable and pretty houses!

Sunset Cliffs, San Diego

We caught the sunset at these cliffs and it was out of this world. The sky was so beautiful and the wind was cooling. We wish we could have stayed longer.

Zion National Park

I just love the colours of the Zion National Park! Don't you? Visiting Zion was truly one of the best experiences I've had in the United States.
